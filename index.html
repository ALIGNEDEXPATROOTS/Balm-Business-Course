From Christine
Hey, I'm Christine, and I'm so glad you're here.

Back in 2016, I started a natural products business from my kitchen in South Africa. I had three simple balms, one of them a lip balm, and zero fancy qualifications beyond a short aromatherapy course (which, for the record, is not necessary to get started). I did all the research myself. I created and tested my own recipes. I designed my own labels, my logo, my brand identity. I built a Facebook page from scratch.

And eventually I walked into natural health retailers and negotiated wholesale deals supplying 20 to 30 units at a time at first, which later became a lot more, and landed my products with a big online retailer called Faithful to Nature.

I'm telling you this not to flex, but so you know: this is doable. Every single step in this course is something I actually did with no special connections, no big budget, and no business background.

This course is built for anyone, anywhere in the world. The principles are the same whether you're in Ireland, Australia, Canada, Kenya, or Kalamazoo. Where the specifics differ (like cosmetic regulations), I'll show you how to find what applies to YOU. Let's build this thing. One honest step at a time.
